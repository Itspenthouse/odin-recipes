no_rise_pizza_dough

Description

This quick pizza dough recipe simply involves a few basic ingredients and patting the dough into the pan for a delicious crust. No need to wait for the dough to rise with the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all_purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Combine flour, sugar, yeast, and salt in a large bowl. Mix in warm water and oil until dough comes together.
